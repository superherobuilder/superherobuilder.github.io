Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Is the app free to download?

Yes, the app is free to download with optional in-app purchases for additional features and unlimited battles.

How do I create a new superhero?

Follow these steps:

  • Tap the "Create" button on the main menu
  • Choose your hero's appearance
  • Give them a unique name
  • Save your creation

How many superheroes can I create?

  • Free version: Limited number of creations with ads
  • Premium version: Unlimited superhero creations

Can I edit my superhero after creating them?

Currently, you cannot edit your superhero's appearance once saved.

How do battle credits work?

  • You start with 5 free credits that can be used to start battles
  • Watch ads to earn more credits
  • Purchase the premium version for unlimited battles

What are the different game modes?

  • Single Player (vs Computer)
  • Two Player (Local multiplayer)

How do I restore my purchases?

Go to Unlock Full Version, then select 'Restore Purchases' to recover any previous premium purchases.

Why did I lose my characters?

Characters are saved locally. If you've:

  • Deleted the app
  • Changed devices
  • Cleared app data

Does the app work offline?

Yes, the app works completely offline except for watching ads and making purchases.

What's included in the premium version?

  • Unlimited battle credits
  • No ads

How do I upgrade to the premium full version?

Tap the "Unlock Full Version" button on the Home Screen.

Why can't I start a battle?

Common reasons:

  • Not enough battle credits
  • Need to create more characters (minimum 2)
  • Network issues when watching ads
  • Device compatibility issues
