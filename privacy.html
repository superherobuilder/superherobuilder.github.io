Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This privacy policy explains our data practices.

The Superhero Builder and Battles app is built as a Freemium app. This SERVICE is initially provided at no cost and is intended for use as is. However, the app does offer in-app purchases.

Data Collection

This app does not collect any personal information directly. All superhero creations are stored locally on your device.

Advertising

We use Google AdMob to display non-personalized advertisements in our app. We have specifically configured our app to serve only non-personalized ads, which means:

  • We do not collect or track any personal data
  • We do not use advertising identifiers
  • We do not collect location data
  • We do not share any data with third parties for tracking purposes

Non-Personalized Ads

  • The ads you see are contextual and not based on your personal data
  • Ads are shown based only on current app context and general location (country level)
  • No personal information is collected or stored
